An Attitude of Gratitude

Published: Mon 02 December 2024
By Laird Streak

In Journal.

tags: Journal

What is an attitude of gratitude you may be asking? Even when all else fails and you are in the depths of dispair remember there is allways hope.
I am in a terrible place at this time due to lack of focus and being un-able to stick to promises made and best efforts I have let someone down.
But even in the worst times there is a rescue God loves me with my flaws and all. This song is a reminder and I hope it can help some-one else also struggling.
