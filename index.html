What I want !!!!!

Published: Thu 18 December 2025
By Laird Streak

In Personal.

tags: Personal

What I Want Now

How I Want to Feel

  • Calm in my own head
  • More in control, less reactive
  • Clear, steady, grounded
  • Quietly proud of myself most days

Health & Energy

  • Easier breathing, lower stress
  • Stable energy (no big spikes or crashes)
  • A better relationship with my body

Habits & Routines

  • Simple routines I can keep
  • Fewer numbing habits, more grounding ones
  • Intentional mornings, calmer evenings

Mindset

  • Less self-criticism
  • Tools that actually help (not feel like work)
  • More self-trust, less overthinking

Relationships & Boundaries

  • Emotionally safe, respectful connections
  • Clear boundaries without guilt
  • Less explaining, more honesty

Direction

  • Clarity on what matters now
  • Progress over perfection
  • Permission to change course

What Iâ€™m Letting Go Of

  • All-or-nothing thinking
  • Avoidance habits
  • Unrealistic standards
  • Waiting for the perfect time

Bottom Line

I want a simpler, calmer life where my actions match what I know is good for me.
