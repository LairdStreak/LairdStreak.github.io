About

Hello! I'm Laird, a passionate software engineer with a love for problem-solving and building innovative tech solutions.

When I'm not working on software projects, you’ll likely find me unwinding with a good game or out on the water enjoying a relaxing day of fishing. Gaming allows me to tap into my creativity, while fishing gives me a peaceful escape to recharge.

Above all, my greatest joy comes from spending time with my three wonderful children. They inspire me every day with their curiosity, energy, and love for life. Whether we're exploring new adventures together or just hanging out, they are the true heart of everything I do.

I’m always excited to connect with like-minded individuals, share ideas, and collaborate on exciting projects. Let’s build something amazing together!
